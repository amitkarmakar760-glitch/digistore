Refund Policy

Digital products work differently from physical goods — here's exactly how refunds and replacements are handled.

Last updated: 23 July 2026

1. Nature of digital goods

Because our products are delivered instantly as downloadable files, purchases are generally final once the file has been delivered. This policy exists to protect against misuse while still covering genuine delivery problems.

2. When a replacement is provided

  • The file is corrupted, incomplete, or won't open.
  • You received the wrong product due to a checkout or listing error.
  • The download link expired before you could access it.

In any of these cases, email your order ID within 7 days of purchase and we'll resend or replace the file at no extra cost.

3. When a refund may be considered

A refund is considered only if a replacement isn't possible — for example, if a course module described in the listing is genuinely missing from the delivered files. Refunds are processed back to the original payment method via Instamojo and can take 5–7 business days to reflect.

4. What isn't covered

  • Change of mind after downloading the file.
  • Not having enough time to use the product.
  • Compatibility issues unrelated to the file itself (e.g. no PDF reader installed).

5. How to request a replacement or refund

Email hello@digistore.in with your order ID and a short description of the issue, or use the contact form. We respond within 24 hours.
