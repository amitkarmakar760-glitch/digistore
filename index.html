FAQ

Good to know before you buy.

How do I receive my file after paying?

Right after your payment is confirmed on Instamojo, you'll get an email with your download link or attached file. Check your spam folder if it doesn't show up within a few minutes.

What payment methods are accepted?

Instamojo supports UPI, debit/credit cards, netbanking, and popular wallets — everything is handled on their secure checkout page.

Can I get a refund?

Digital products are generally non-refundable once delivered, but if a file is corrupted or missing, we'll replace it within 7 days — just email us your order ID.

Do I need an account to buy?

No account needed. Just click Buy, pay, and your product arrives by email.
