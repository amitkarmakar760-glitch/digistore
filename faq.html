Frequently asked questions

Everything about buying, downloading, and getting support at DigiStore.

How do I receive my file after paying?

Right after your payment is confirmed on Instamojo, you'll get an email with your download link or attached file. Check your spam folder if it doesn't show up within a few minutes.

What payment methods are accepted?

Instamojo supports UPI, debit/credit cards, netbanking, and popular wallets — everything is handled on their secure checkout page.

Can I get a refund?

Digital products are generally non-refundable once delivered, but if a file is corrupted or missing, we'll replace it within 7 days — just email us your order ID. See our full Refund Policy.

Do I need an account to buy?

No account needed. Just click Buy Now, pay, and your product arrives by email.

Is my payment information safe?

Yes. All payments are processed by Instamojo directly — DigiStore never sees or stores your card, UPI, or banking details.

Can I use a product on more than one device?

Yes. Once downloaded, the file is yours to use across your own personal devices for lifetime access.

What if I never receive my download email?

First check spam or promotions folders. If it's still missing after 15 minutes, message us on WhatsApp or email with your order ID and we'll resend it.

Do bundles cost less than buying items separately?

Yes — every bundle is priced lower than the sum of its individual items, shown as the strikethrough price on the card.

Are course videos downloadable or streamed?

Course files are delivered as downloadable video files (MP4) plus any accompanying PDFs, so you can watch offline.

How do I contact support?

Email hello@digistore.in, message us on WhatsApp, or use the contact form — we reply within 24 hours.
