Privacy Policy

What we collect, why we collect it, and how it's protected.

Last updated: 23 July 2026

1. Information we collect

When you contact us or subscribe to our newsletter, we collect the details you provide directly, such as your name and email address. When you make a purchase, payment processing and any billing details are handled entirely by Instamojo — DigiStore does not collect or store your card, UPI, or banking information.

2. How we use your information

  • To deliver the digital product you purchased.
  • To respond to support requests sent by email, WhatsApp, or the contact form.
  • To send occasional product updates if you've subscribed to the newsletter.

3. Third-party services

Payments are processed by Instamojo under its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing Instamojo's terms directly for details on how payment data is handled.

4. Cookies and local storage

This site uses your browser's local storage to remember preferences such as dark mode and wishlist items. No tracking cookies are used, and no data is sold to third parties.

5. Data retention

Contact form submissions and newsletter emails are retained only as long as needed to provide support or the requested updates, and are deleted on request.

6. Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing hello@digistore.in.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated terms.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@digistore.in.
